Frequently Asked Questions

1. What makes Imperator boxing gloves different?

Every pair is handcrafted from full‑grain leather and triple‑stitched for durability. We use multi‑layer foam that balances power distribution and wrist support, reducing hand injuries during intense training.

2. Do you offer custom glove sizes or colours?

Yes. We stock 10 oz, 12 oz, 14 oz, and 16 oz gloves in standard colours, and you can order custom padding weight, colourway, and personalised embroidery. Turn‑around time for custom gloves is typically 3 weeks.

3. What training programmes do you provide?

We run beginner Box‑Fit classes, intermediate technical sessions, and advanced sparring clinics. Personal 1‑to‑1 coaching is available on request, focusing on fight‑specific conditioning, footwork, and pad work.

4. How do I book a class?

Go to ourtimetable page, choose your school, and click an available slot. You’ll receive a confirmation e‑mail as soon as you book.

5. Can I return or exchange gloves?

Unused stock gloves can be exchanged within 30 days. Custom gloves are made to order and cannot be returned unless there is a manufacturing defect. Contact customer support with your order number and we’ll guide you through the process.
